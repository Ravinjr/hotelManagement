Kataragama Dewala

“Kataragama” is one of South Asia's most significant multi-religious places of worship, with more than 2,500 years of written history. Kataragama is a holy place sanctified by the touch of Lord Buddha's feet and where Kataragama Divine King is alive, and which is immensely respected by Rajaraja Mahamatyadin and Hudi people led by Dutugemunu Maharaja.

The annual festivals of Ruhunu Maha Kataragama Temple are the Year Festival, The pole-cutting festival or cup-planting, the Esala Maha Festival and the Il Maha Festival are held as annual festivals according to the previous royal decree even today.
